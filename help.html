Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start learning to code?

Start with our free tutorials! We recommend beginning with HTML & CSS, then moving to JavaScript. Check out our "Getting Started" category above for curated learning paths.

Is VictorNewb completely free?

Yes! All our tutorials, code examples, and learning resources are completely free. We believe in making coding education accessible to everyone.

How can I get help with my code?

You can use our Live Chat feature (click the button in the top nav) to chat with our community or support team. You can also browse our tutorial sections for solutions.

What programming languages do you cover?

We currently cover HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, and Java. We're constantly adding new content based on user requests!

Can I contribute to VictorNewb?

Absolutely! We welcome contributors. Email us at victornewb@support.com or reach out via Live Chat to learn how you can contribute tutorials or code examples.
